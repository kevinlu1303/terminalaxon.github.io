About
Before the violent, ill-advised, and somehow successful removal of Louisville's municipality, 3 disaffected ne'er-do-wells set out to galvanize the public into an uprising; One that was predominately led by Louisville youth. John, the derelict blacksmith, whose funkadelic charisma proved unrivaled. Colin, the intellectual giant, and supreme grounding force, and Hawthorne, their bard-like leader.
